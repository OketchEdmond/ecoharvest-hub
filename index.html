CONNECTING NAIROBI WITH THE FRESHEST LOCAL HARVESTS

GET STARTED

EcoHarvest Hub was founded by Edmond Oketch to address the gap between small-scale farmers and consumers in Nairobi, where fresh produce often went to waste due to lack of market access. Through the CropConnect App, farmers can list produce directly for consumers to buy, while the Agri-Insights Dashboard provides real-time market data, weather updates, and sustainable farming tips. The platform reduces food waste, supports farmers, and ensures communities access fresh, affordable, locally sourced food—building a more sustainable food system in Kenya.
